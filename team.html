About Us

PlateAI empowers Tanzanians to make informed choices that lead to healthier dietary habits. In a region where unhealthy dietary patterns are widespread, characterized by repetitive and monotonous diets, we are committed to bring diversity and flexibility with meal recommendations tailored to individual needs.
